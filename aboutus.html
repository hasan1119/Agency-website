Commercial ceilings such as vinyl,acoustical and metal ceilings that are in warehouses and cooking areas build up a lot of soil. These soils are made up of different types of grease that cause the ceilings to discolor.Often times we will see ceilings appear darker or yellow out in areas where there is cooking or some form of combustion occurring

Often times painting or replacing these ceiling surfaces are more costly then simply cleaning them. With the proper maintenance program, these hard to reach areas can be addressed accordingly and maintained through the proper cleaning frequency. If left unmaintained, these ceiling surfaces become an eye sore, and even a concern for inspection especially for the food industry.

To protect your investment, ceilings should be professionally cleaned periodically to prevent any damage from daily use. At Max Cleaning Service Inc, we provide professional ceiling cleaning restoration services to bring back the original beauty and luster of your ceiling's surface.
